# App Store Screenshot Plan

Use a consistent iPhone Pro Max simulator/device, clean demo data, English and Turkish sets, and no real personal or patient data.

## Required Story

1. **Skin awareness, assisted by AI**
   - Screen: Welcome/home.
   - Caption: “Build better skin awareness.”

2. **Capture a clear skin-area photo**
   - Screen: Image upload with camera/gallery choices.
   - Caption: “Start with a clear photo.”

3. **Add helpful context**
   - Screen: Symptoms/context step.
   - Caption: “Add optional context for your assessment.”

4. **Review possible findings**
   - Screen: Result card showing “Possible Finding” and “Model Confidence Score.”
   - Caption: “Review AI-assisted possible findings.”

5. **Track visible changes**
   - Screen: Lesion/skin-area tracking timeline.
   - Caption: “Monitor visible changes over time.”

6. **Keep your history organised**
   - Screen: History.
   - Caption: “Keep awareness reports in one place.”

## Screenshot Rules

- Do not use “diagnose,” “detect cancer,” “clinical accuracy,” or treatment claims.
- Keep the medical limitation visible where practical.
- Use synthetic/demo images and fictional profile information.
- Do not show incomplete RevenueCat pricing until StoreKit products are configured.
- Verify text does not clip at larger accessibility font sizes.
