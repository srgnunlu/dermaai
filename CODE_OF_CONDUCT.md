# Code of Conduct

DermaAssistAI is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone who participates in the project.

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1. By participating, you are expected to uphold this code.

## Our standards

Be kind, respectful, and constructive. Assume good faith, welcome newcomers, and
keep discussion focused on what is best for the project and the people it serves.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to
the maintainer, [@srgnunlu](https://github.com/srgnunlu). All reports will be
reviewed and handled confidentially.

## Full text

The complete standards, scope, and enforcement guidelines are available in the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
